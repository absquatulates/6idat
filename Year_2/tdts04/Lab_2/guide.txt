Proxy

1. Set your browsers HTTP proxy to use 
    IP: 127.0.0.1
    Port: 1337

2. Run proxy.py in your terminal
    python3 proxy.py

3. Load an HTTP website, if the website contains the words 
   "Stockholm" and/or "smiley" they will be changed.


Notes:
Most of the testing was done on campus with private computer
it works fine there but we ran in to some problems on some of
the campus computers due to firewall issues.
